Who we are

BestBud started as a data science community. We are your friends on the internet. We help each other learn about data and programming for better job opportunities. As of today, we're growing into an internet startup offering services to our remote learning community.

See what we're working on

The Data Organization

This is where it all started.
Our facebook page launched in May 2017, originally named "Data Dog and Friends". In a few months, we accumulated 350+ followers.
The Shiba Inu photos were our silly effort to make data science look and feel more friendly.
Later we would learn that there's another company named DataDog. We also wanted to make it sound more professional. So the name was changed to The Data Organization.

Data Science for Startups

Initially created as a learning group for Data Dog and Friends followers under the name "Fullstack Data Developers". At some point, many people from India and the U.S. joined to look for developers and technical cofounders.
So we decided to change the name to "Data Science for Startups".

EcoScan App

EcoScan is an award-winning AI-enabled application for nutrition planning. It can detect various types of foods live using a phone camera. The app was initially developed by our team together with 4 incredible friends at Global Hackathon in Jeju.

Creating Minds

A crazy 2018 brought us to Korea to bootstrap our business for 4 months. We got accepted to Y Combinator Startup School for their 10-week online course and received $30K from DigitalOcean, a YC partner to kickstart our data platform project.

TheDataist

TheDataist is a combination of what we have learned from previous ideas. It started in early 2019. Now we have 100+ followers on Medium and 300+ members on Facebook.
